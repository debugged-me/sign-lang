Signing "Bonjour" (Hello)

Module Progress: 65%
0:12 / 0:45
Side View
1

Hand Shape

Flat hand, fingers together, thumb tucked slightly against the palm.

2

Starting Point

Touch the fingertips of your dominant hand to your forehead or temple.

3

The Movement

Move the hand outward and slightly downward in a small arc motion.

warning

error Common Mistakes

  • cancel

    Wrist Tension: Don't keep your wrist too stiff; let the movement flow naturally from the elbow.

  • cancel

    Placement: Avoid starting the sign from your chest; "Bonjour" always originates near the head.
